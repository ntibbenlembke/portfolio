About Me

In 2022, I worked as a Software Engineering Intern with Publicis Sapient, where I was tasked with developing a user-friendly website for efficient image distribution across numerous store locations. To learn more, check out my projects

I also have extensive experience as an audio engineer, after I ran the Tech staff at the Lion's Pause, a student-run performance venue. Here, I trained students to use industry-standard equipment (Yamaha QL5, LS9, Behringer X32, Full Boar 4), and communicated with touring artists to execute their vision in our space. After graduation, I went on tour with the Sacramento Mandarins as an audio engineer, where I mixed live audio during stadium performances (on A&H SQ7).

When I’m not at the computer, I’m usually making music with friends, working on graphic design projects, or rock climbing.
